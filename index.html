About the game

Help Arno reach the stars!
Arno is just an average satyr who, after the rejection on the part of Dan and Dionysus, is trying to get the approval of the Gods. For this purpose, he needs to get the blessed wings that will make him reach their world!
Please, help Arno. With your help, he will be happy again!

How To Play

Use or to move left.
Use or to move right.
Use or to jump.
Lightning bolts are bad for Arno.
He's an hydrophobic, so keep him out of the water!
Have fun reaching the stars!
